About Pokémon Card Tracker

Welcome to the Pokémon Card Tracker! This application allows you to search for Pokémon trading cards and view their pricing information sourced from the Pokémon TCG API.

Features

  • Search for Pokémon cards by name and set.
  • View average, low, high, and market prices for each card.
  • Access live listings on TCGplayer for the most up-to-date prices.

Data Source

The pricing data is fetched from the Pokémon TCG API, which provides live information about Pokémon trading cards.
